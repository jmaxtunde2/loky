    What is Anomaly In cryptocurrency ?

    Anomaly detection is a technique used to identify unusual patterns that do not conform to expected behavior, known as anomalies or outliers. These anomalies can indicate critical incidents, such as potential fraud, technical glitches, or significant shifts in data patterns.

    Imagine a cryptocurrency typically trades with a stable volume and gradual price changes. Suddenly, there is a significant spike in trading volume and a sharp increase in price. This anomaly could be due to:

    • Market Manipulation: A coordinated effort to artificially inflate the price.
    • Insider Trading: Someone with insider knowledge making significant trades based on non-public information.
    • News Event: A major news event affecting the cryptocurrency market.

    Detecting such anomalies can alert traders to investigate further and make informed decisions, such as holding off on buying into a pump-and-dump scheme or selling off assets before a suspected market crash.
